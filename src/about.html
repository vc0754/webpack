About

SINCERO GROUP was found in 2008, thriving on our ultimate corporate ideal-“Optimized for life happiness”, the driving principle behind our practices.

SINCERO Group core businesses are Retail, Healthcare, Online and Manufacturing.

SINCERO Global Industrial (M) SDN. BHD. is a wholly owned manufacturing arm of SINCERO GROUP.

We are engaged in home appliances product development, production and sales. We mainly produces induction cooker, ceramic cooker and electric cooker combination furnace.

To date we have served more than 100 businesses from various countries in SEA and the list keep increasing. Our manufacturing standard are up to the ISO 9001 quality management system requirements throughout the entire production process, always strive to achieve zero defect products.

High quality, excellent services, good credit enable us to establish good relationship with customers from all over the world.

In future, we are also committed to improve the management innovation and technical innovation to meet the various requirements of our customers. We desire to establish a beneficial and long-term relationship with you, All OEM and ODM projects are always welcome!
